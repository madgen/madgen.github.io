This is Mistral Contrastin's personal website. I am a Ph.D. student at Department of Computer Science and Technology, University of Cambridge. Here you can find some information about my research, teaching as well as technical and cultural rumblings.

My research is in programming languages and verification. I worked on static analysis of Fortran (chill, using Haskell). I am now working on a Datalog variant for querying data under version control. Also I am an ex-Rubyist, up-and-coming Haskeller, and wannabe Agda programmer.

If I am not in front of my computer in the nearest hip café or chasing the hottest art films that I barely understand, I can be found running mid distance by the River Cam.
